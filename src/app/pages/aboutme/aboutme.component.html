About me

Hello, I'm Sergio Toledo, a software engineer with a strong background in Control and Automation Engineering. I recently graduated from Georgian College's Computer Programming program and am currently working as a Software Engineer, where I focus on full-stack development, creating innovative solutions that enhance efficiency and make a meaningful impact.

My journey in technology is rooted in curiosity and a drive to bridge the gap between engineering and practical problem-solving. I transitioned from a production management role, where I honed my understanding of industrial workflows, to software development, motivated by the potential to craft tools that streamline operations and foster innovation.

During my 8-month internship with Ontario Power Generation, I worked on projects that transformed business process monitoring and management. I developed web applications and data analytics tools, led the creation of a Project Charter for a major renovation project, automated operational reminders using Python, and leveraged Power BI to turn complex data into actionable insights.
